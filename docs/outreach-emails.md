## Base email (core shared by all org variants)

**Subject:** A 12-minute game about debt bondage, launching July 30

Hi,

I'm a software engineer based in NYC.

A while ago, I watched a Business Insider documentary on
bonded labor at Pakistan's brick kilns. It had a powerful effect on me, so I built a game about it.

Game: https://indenturedgame.com/

It's called Indentured Servitude — a free browser game, about 12 minutes long. You
shovel mud, mold bricks, fire them, and try to pay down a debt that only grows. It's
rigged; you can't win. The only way out is a button that says "Accept your fate." The
end screen then explains that the mechanics you just experienced are real, and points
players to organizations fighting this problem.

I'm launching it July 30, the World Day Against Trafficking in Persons. Two asks: if
you think it's useful for awareness work, share it. And if you have any feedback before launch, I would love to hear it.
